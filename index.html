About Me

Arun works as a Machine Learning Engineer at Deep Forest Sciences. Arun received his MSc degree in Data Science from PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. During his free time, Arun contributes to the DeepChem project. The DeepChem project is an open-source project which aims to democratize AI for scientific applications. Arun helps on building tools for representing scientific dataset in suitables ways for use in machine learning applications, building deep learning models with different architectures and benchmarking them on scientific datasets. The other hobbies of Arun are reading literature works and studying privacy related implications of big data.
