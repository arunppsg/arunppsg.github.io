About Me

Arun received his MSc degree in Data Science from PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. During the day time, Arun works as a project assistant at IIT-Madras on cybersecurity, mainly focusing on detecting malwares in network traffic. During his free time, Arun contributes to the DeepChem project. The DeepChem project is an open-source project which aims to democratize AI for scientific applications. Arun helps on building tools for representing scientific dataset in suitables ways for use in machine learning applications, building deep learning models with different architectures and benchmarking them on scientific datasets. The other hobbies of Arun are reading literature works and studying privacy related implications of big data. The long-term mission of Arun is to make knowledge and education widely and freely accessible to all. To this end, he support charities like Agaram Foundation, Wikimedia and a couple more.
