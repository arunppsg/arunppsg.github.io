For some reason, I liked this guy - MK Stalin.
First, his dad who was born in Thiruvarur, the same place as me, then him.
The liking for Stalin and Karunanidhi was irrational at first - only due to the fact they were from the same place as me.

Stalin's oath taking ceremony was a goosebumps moment to me.
Ever since he was born, the throne was within hands reach to him but it evaded him for 68 years.
And for these many years, he was there patiently waiting, watching it.
He become CM at the age of 68 on May 7, 2021.

I liked two chief ministers of Tamil Nadue.
My most favorite is Kamarajar who pioneered the revolutionary mid-day meal scheme in Tamil Nadu.
The next is MK Stalin's, for his secular government in the time of divisive politics.

After his first term as chief minister, he lost the election in his own constituency and for me, it was very sad to see this.
This image - [Statement of a Statesman](https://www.thehindu.com/photos/statement-of-a-statesman/article70952613.ece), pushed me to the verge of the tears.
Kolathur is his constituency.
He visited it every week during his tenure as CM, brought in new hospitals, upgraded schools and libraries and did other infrastructure development.
And he lost the election in his own home ground.
So was other developments and industries brought to Tamil Nadu, but he lost the state election due to the Vijay wave.

Vijay's rise may feel that it is a sudden and quick rise - but no, it is decades of planning and patience with a clear focus towards the vision.
Vijay had the long-term vision.
On the top-level, he launched his party and won elections in a gap of two years.
But behind this is many years of preparation of the grounds for the battle, right from the state of his initial movie days.

His success can be explained via these two Kurals.

1. Time - ஞாலம் கருதினுங் கைகூடுங் காலம் கருதி இடத்தாற் செயின் (Kural 484)

    Valluvar says that with the right time, even the world could be won with ease.

    Vijay was at his peak of career and in the Tamil cinema industry.
    In the state politics, the time was ripe for a generational shift with the absence of erstwhile stalwarts J Jayalalitha and M Karunanidhi.
    The leadership vaccum along with the mass attention and connect which he had with people made it a right time for him to enter politics.

2. Deliberate Planning - சூழ்ச்சி முடிவு துணிவெய்தல் அத்துணிவு தாழ்ச்சியுள் தங்குதல் தீது (Kural 671)


    Vijay planned about his entry to politics decades ago.
    Be it organisaing his fans club into a welfare association or the social topics he addressed in his movies or lending support to social causes in Tamil Nadu.
    These events added momentum to his entry in politics and finally culminated him becoming Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu.

There are other things which I liked about this election:

- the party's rise came without any cash for votes
- I believed that to succeed in politics, one must be an extrovert but Vijay broke that belief.


Now that he has the attention of the masses, many of whome are millenials and youngsters, I hope that he turns the attention into something more meaningful and useful for the society.

Stalin did not fall, it is simply like a wildfire sweeping through the state.

For Stalin,
```
சிதைவிடத்து ஒல்கார் உரவோர் புதையம்பிற்
பட்டுப்பா டூன்றுங் களிறு.
```
an elephant pierced by a shower of arrow only takes a stronger firm, the strong minded will not quail in the face of defeat.

I hope that, Stalin, like the sun will rise again.
