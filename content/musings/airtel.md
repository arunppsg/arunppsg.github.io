date = 2026-06-07
+++

My mobile number ends with `14`.
On a Friday night, while recharging my mobile, I recharged a wrong number.
Instead of my number which ends in `14`, I recharged `13`.

I opened a ticket with Airtel for refund on April 15, 2026 for wrong recharge.
Airtel, being Airtel, turned down the request saying we can't reverse the transaction.
I raised it to the appellate in Airtel, the response was the same.

On 23rd April, I registered a complaint in the [Public Grievance Portal](https://pgportal.gov.in/).
On 30th April, Airtel said it will resolve in 48-72 hours but it did not resolve.

I did an appeal in the PGPortal raising the complaint to the nodal officer.
I took a while but on 1st June, I got a refund from Airtel.

Steps to get a refund:
1. Raise a ticket - Airtel will deny refund.
2. Raise it to Airtel appellate authority - Airtel will deny refund.
3. Raise a ticket in PGPortal - Airtel will say it will refund but it will not refund.
4. Raise a ticket to nodal officer in PGPortal - Airtel will do the refund.
