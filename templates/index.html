Arun Pa Thiagarajan

I am Arun. I work on ML + Science space. I am a founding engineer at Deep Forest Sciences. I work on building Chiron - a platform for accelerating scientific discovery using machine learning. To this end, I contribute to platform design, its efficiency and scalability as well as operations. I also contribute to DeepChem, building open source machine learning algorithms for applications in drug discovery, helping in project maintenance.

Before Deep Forest, I worked as a project assistant at RISE Lab, Indian Institute of Technology, Madras for about a year where my work focused on detecting malwares from network traffic using predictive algorithms. I graduated with a Masters degree in Data Science from PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore in May 2021. I like to read, swim and practice yoga during my spare time and I care about socio-economic development and language development. I volunteer at FOSS United - a non-profit foundation that aims at promoting and strengthening the Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) ecosystem in India.
